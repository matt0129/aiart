# AI ART


As I am both an Artificial Intelligence and Art & Design enthusiast, I was naturally gravitated to the realm of AI-generated art. I was further introduced to this area of art during my internship at 500px. Here is a collection of my own AI art pieces, generated from scratch with no inital image using Disco Diffusion v5.4 with some in-house tweaks. Enjoy!
